Who We Are

BRIN Cisitu Bandung is a leading research institution under Indonesia’s National Research and Innovation Agency (BRIN). Located in Bandung, we are dedicated to advancing scientific research and innovation to support Indonesia’s development and global competitiveness.

Our team of researchers, scientists, and innovators work collaboratively across various disciplines to address national challenges, foster sustainable growth, and contribute to the global knowledge economy.

Our Mission

To conduct cutting-edge research and develop innovative solutions that drive Indonesia’s progress in science, technology, and sustainable development.

Our Vision

To be a world-class research institution that empowers Indonesia through transformative innovations and impactful discoveries.

Our Commitment

At BRIN Cisitu Bandung, we are committed to fostering collaboration between academia, industry, and government to create a thriving ecosystem for research and innovation. We aim to deliver solutions that address real-world challenges while promoting inclusivity, sustainability, and excellence.
